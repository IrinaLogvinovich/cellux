About FLASHING CASE

Our unique flashing phone case lights up when exposed to sound. Light animation is a pattern of light that travels across the image according to sound dynamics. The image will illuminate differently depending on sound level and sound activity.

Flashing case is powered by its own internal rechargeable battery that supports a long lasting illuminating mode. When sound is not present illuminating mode enters a sleep phase within 2 seconds. When sound returns illuminating mode will re-active within 2 seconds. This feature greatly increases battery life keeping the image flashing for several days before charging. The battery can be recharged as fast as 15-20 minutes using magnetic USB cord provided. Illuminating mode has on/off sensor and can be activated with the touch of your finger.

Flashing Case is durable, light weight, water resistant and made of non-toxic materials. It is very easy to attach to your phone as well as detach.
